How is the AI Score calculated?
🔍

We read the reviews so you don't have to

We fetch up to 100 recent reviews from Google and send them to an AI — the same kind that powers ChatGPT and Gemini.

🤖

AI spots the fakes

The AI reads every review and asks: does this sound like a real person, or does it sound scripted, copy-pasted, or paid for? It looks for patterns — like 20 reviews all saying "best salon in Gurgaon, must visit!", or reviews that unnaturally use the full business name or staff names. Real people don't write like that. Any sudden spike in reviews during a particular time period is also flagged.

⭐

It gives a fair score

After ignoring the fake reviews, the AI estimates what rating the place actually deserves, based only on reviews it believes are real.
